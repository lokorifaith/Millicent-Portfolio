About Advocate Millicent Lokaale

Millicent Lokaale is an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya and a Commissioner for Oaths dedicated to delivering excellent legal services guided by integrity, justice, and professionalism.

Her passion for law and commitment to community service drives her to provide fair and practical solutions for individuals and organizations seeking justice and legal guidance.
